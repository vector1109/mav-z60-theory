# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository is intended to support rigorous mathematical, computational, and reproducible work.

Discussion should remain constructive, technical, and respectful.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- engage in good faith,
- focus on technical substance,
- provide reproducible arguments where applicable,
- distinguish speculation from formal claims,
- remain respectful in disagreement.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- personal attacks,
- harassment,
- bad-faith disruption,
- misrepresentation of results,
- hostile or non-technical escalation,
- deliberate degradation of reproducibility or documentation.

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- issues,
- pull requests,
- discussions,
- repository-linked communication.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards.

The goal is to preserve a technically rigorous and usable research environment.
